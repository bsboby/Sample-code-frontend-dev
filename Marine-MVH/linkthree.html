Linkthree is a free new venue finding agency startup,
that helps you organize your business event from a-z!

the project

The client came to the agency for the development of their brand identity and the creation of a web site

Linkthree is a venue agency who offers to find the best place and partners to make client’s event’s successful. Their advantage against their competitor’s, is that they save both time and money to client’s, since Linkthree is compensated by the service providers.

The client’s main objective, is for the users to research easily and precisly, hand picked, hotels and events.

my role

my part in the project was to design the graphic look and interface for the web site

From the structure to the look and feel, details, image picking,.. I was aiming for a beautiful, simple, modern design that would transpire Linkthree’s values and professional image. To achieve the clients objective, I designed a simple and precise search bar, that users can always find in the header. It makes it easy to multiply the number of searches thanks to its accessibility at mouse click !
